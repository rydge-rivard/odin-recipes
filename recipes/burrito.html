Chicken Burrito

Description

Chicken Burrito Bowls are a healthy crowd pleasing favorite! Tender chunks of chicken are dressed in a zesty taco lime seasoning with black beans and rice.

Add in cilantro for a fresh and peppery kick and your favorite toppings like salsa and guacamole!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Olive Oil
  • 4 Cups White Rice
  • 1 Cup Salsa
  • 1 Lime
  • 4 Cups Lettuce (chopped)

Steps

  1. Combine diced chicken breast and taco seasoning and toss to coat the chicken.
  2. Heat olive oil over medium-high heat in a 12" skillet. Add the chicken and cook until no pink remains. Sprinkle with lime juice, stir, and remove from heat.
  3. Combine cooked rice and cilantro in a bowl and stir to combine.
  4. To serve, fill 4 bowls with equal amounts of lettuce, rice, black beans, chicken, salsa, sour cream, avocado, and cheese.
